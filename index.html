Interesting Facts About Origami

  1. Origami is the art of paper-folding
  2. Its name derives from Japanese words ori (“folding”) and kami (“paper”)
  3. Traditional origami consists of folding a single sheet of square paper
  4. It is made into a sculpture without cutting, gluing, taping, or even marking it
  5. Origami has been proven to improve concentration
